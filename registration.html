Publication

Publication

  • All submissions will be considered for publication in UGC CARE and International Journals with DOI numbers.
  • All accepted papers will be published in conference proceedings. Selected papers will be available online on Institute Research Journal - “Vishwakarma Journal Of Engineering Tecnology” (http://www.vjer.in/) ISSN: 2456-8465.
  • Selected papers will be published in Scopus Indexed Journals with an additional fee of Rs 8500/- based on journal selection.
  • Authors can contact the organizing team for additional information of Scopus paper submissions. Journals for Scopus- IJITEE, IJRTE, IJEAT.
